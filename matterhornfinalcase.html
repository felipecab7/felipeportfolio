About the project

Empowering the ASL community
Matterhorn's Online Dispute Resolution (ODR) platform was intended to facilitate dispute resolution between two parties without having to go to court. Matterhorn's platform was observed low sign-up conversion rates among American Sign Language users (ASL). The platform was found to be complex and inaccessible which increased the risk of losing incoming ASL users.
How might we create compelling solutions so that more American Sign Language users successfully complete Matterhorn's ODR registration flow?
